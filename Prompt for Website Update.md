Maintain all existing CSS, layout structure, color scheme, typography, spacing, animations, and visual styling. Only modify text content and content structure.

### 2. Content Simplification
Remove all specific metrics, campaign names, company references, revenue figures, and personal achievement stories (e.g., "#FlipTheTurf campaign", "3.95 billion impressions", "$250MM brand", specific company names).

### 3. Core Capabilities to Highlight
Derived from resume, focus on:

- Maintain professional, consultative tone without promotional language
